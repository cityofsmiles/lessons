n-Factorial: the product of the positive integer $n$ and all the positive integers less than $n$. 
 
\vspce 
For any natural number $n$,   
\[
n! = n(n-1)(n-2)\ldots (3)\cdot(2)\cdot(1). 
\]

\vspce 

For the number 0,
\[
0!=1.
\] 

}
