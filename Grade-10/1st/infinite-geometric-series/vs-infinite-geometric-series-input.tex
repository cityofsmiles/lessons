\textbf{Infinite Geometric Series}
\end{center}

\vspace*{1ex}

\textbf{Infinite Geometric Series:} a series of the form $a+ar+ar^{2}+ar^{3}+... +ar^{n-1}+... $

\vspce


To find the sum of an infinite geometric sequence, use

%\begin{center}
$$\displaystyle S = \frac{a}{1-r}, \hspce -1<r<1$$
%\end{center}
