\begin{center}
\textbf{Harmonic Sequence 
}
\end{center}

\textbf{Harmonic Sequence:} a sequence of numbers whose reciprocals form an arithmetic sequence 

\vspce

In symbols, 

\vspce

\begin{center}
$\displaystyle  \frac{1}{a_1},   \frac{1}{a_1 + d}, \frac{1}{a_1 + 2d},..., \frac{1}{a_1 + (n-1)d}$ 
\end{center}
