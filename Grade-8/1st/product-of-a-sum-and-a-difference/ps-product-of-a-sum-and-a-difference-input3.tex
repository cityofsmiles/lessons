\textbf{Problem Set}

\vspce

Multiply the following binomials.
\begin{enumerate}
\item \hspce $(3n-p)(3n+p)$
\item  \hspce $(b+4cd^{2})(b-4cd^{2})$
\item  \hspce $(3a+2bc^{2})(3a-2bc^{2})$
\item  \hspce $(\frac{4}{5}c-\frac{3}{4}d)	(\frac{4}{5}c+\frac{3}{4}d)	$
\item  \hspce $(-3m-2np^{3})(-3m+2np^{3})$
\end{enumerate}
