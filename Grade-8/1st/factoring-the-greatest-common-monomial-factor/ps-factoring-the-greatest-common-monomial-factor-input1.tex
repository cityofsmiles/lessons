\textbf{Practice Exercises}

\vspce

Factor the following polynomials completely. 

\begin{enumerate}
\item \hspce $3x + 6$
\item \hspce $12x^{4} + 8x^{3}y + 4x^{2}y^{2}$
\item \hspce $3x^{3} - 6x^{2} + 3x$
\item \hspce $6y^{3}z + 7y^{2}z^{2} + 2yz^{3}$
\item \hspce $6 x^{3} - 8 x^{2} y^{2} + 4 x^{2} z$
\end{enumerate}
