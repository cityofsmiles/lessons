\noindent\begin{minipage}{\textwidth}
{
\textbf{Linear Function:} a function whose graph is a slant line. It is in the form of $f(x) = mx +b$ or $y= mx + b$ where: 
\begin{itemize} 
\item[\textbullet] $y$ is the dependent variable; 
\item[\textbullet] $x$ is the independent variable which we manipulate to get different results of $y$; 
\item[\textbullet] $m$ is the slope of
the line; 
\item[\textbullet] $b$ is the constant term or the y-intercept.
\end{itemize}  
}
\end{minipage}}
